AKTO Map

about

The objective of this project was to create a map in the style of the New York Subway for the second floor of AKTO's Thessaloniki location. I drew inspiration from the existing NY Subway map but aimed to infuse a personal touch. I chose to design the map in the form of an electrical diagram while keeping the essence of the NY Subway map's style.
