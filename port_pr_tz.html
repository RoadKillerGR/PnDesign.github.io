Tazo Tea

about

The objective of this project was to develop a new concept and redesign the packaging for Tazo Tea's Giant Peach. My concept revolved around a character who founded a religion centered on the worship of fruit. For the redesign, my inspiration drew from various forms of existing religious art, including stained glass and carved imagery, incorporating intricate details. I reimagined the bottle label, bottle cap, and the box for the bottle itself.
Year - 2023
