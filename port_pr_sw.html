Star Wars Poster

about

The objective of this project was to redesign the poster for Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope. I aimed to strike a balance between a modern and retro design, drawing significant inspiration and design cues from the posters of the Star Wars Prequel Trilogy while retaining certain elements from the original poster, such as the logo and vibrant colors.
Year - 2022
