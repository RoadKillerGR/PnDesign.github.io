Lithos & Estia Wine

about

The objective of this project was to design a matching set of two wine bottle labels: one for a white wine named "Λίθος" and one for a red wine named "Εστία." I drew inspiration from various wine bottle labels and abstract art. My aim was to depict in my illustrations the imagery that comes to mind when I encounter the words "Λίθος" and "Εστία," while also adhering to a modern, minimalist design approach.
Year - 2022
