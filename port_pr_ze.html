Zenith Stationery

about

The objective of this project was to revamp the stationery for a company of our choice, and I selected Zenith Power & Gas. We were required to work within the company's existing color palette and utilize their logo. I aimed to craft a fluid and striking design, employing the company's vibrant color scheme and drawing inspiration from the design elements present in the company's logo. I redesigned the stationery set, which included the envelope, business card, and letterhead.
