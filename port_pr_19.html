COVID-19 Infographic

about

The objective of this project was to create an infographic about the effects of COVID-19 in Greece in 2020. We had no restrictions on the information we could present as long as it was accurate and relevant to the project. I drew inspiration from other COVID-19-related infographics worldwide, particularly from the work of Panos Konstantopoulos.
