Deep Sea

about

The objective of this project was to create an application of our own design and present its capabilities and features, while extensively utilizing Figma for the first time. I developed an application focused on identifying and learning about rare sea creatures, incorporating social networking features. My application drew significant inspiration, both in terms of its features and UI design, from existing applications with similar capabilities, such as "PictureThis."
