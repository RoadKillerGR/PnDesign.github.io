EDM Festival Posters

about

The objective of this project was to design a set of three music festival posters using three different design methods. We had the freedom to choose any music festival, whether real or imaginary, and any music genre we desired. I chose to envision an EDM Festival set in Thessaloniki, Greece. My primary source of inspiration came from artworks of other EDM Festivals worldwide. The first poster emphasizes shapes, the second poster focuses on creating shapes using typography, and the third poster highlights vibrant colors and abstract shapes.
Year - 2023
