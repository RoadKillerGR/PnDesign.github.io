Star Beans Coffee

about

The objective of this project was to create a label for coffee beans for a company we established. The theme for the design was "psychedelic." Since psychedelic design was at its peak during the 1960s, I decided to draw design inspiration from another prominent movement of that era, pop art. I aimed to blend these two influences to produce something vibrant and highly distinctive.
