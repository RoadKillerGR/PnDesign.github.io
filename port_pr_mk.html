Cinema Museum of Thessaloniki

about

The objective of this project was to revamp the brand identity and create merchandise for a selected museum, and I opted for the Cinema Museum of Thessaloniki. The initial challenge involved redesigning the museum's logo, and after proposing numerous designs, the chosen logo represented a cinema projection—a simple yet highly effective choice. Subsequently, I developed merchandise designs inspired by various aspects of cinema, including clapperboard coasters featuring movie references, film reel keychains, camera lens do not disturb signs, and an array of cinema-related t-shirts. Additionally, we were tasked with designing a promotional trifold, in which I incorporated design elements from the world of cinema, such as the film leader.
