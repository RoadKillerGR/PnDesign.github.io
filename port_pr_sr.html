Galaxy Race

about

The objective of this project was to create our own board game. I chose to design a simple game reminiscent of snakes and ladders, heavily inspired by an old board game I had access to. For the illustrations, I drew inspiration from various flat design artworks available online. All of the board game's illustrations were hand-drawn by me in Adobe Illustrator.
Year - 2023
