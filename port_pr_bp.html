Èmeraude Pasta

about

The objective of this project was to create a bio pasta manufacturer from scratch, including developing a backstory, concept, and designing packaging for three distinct pasta types, a promotional trifold, and an advertisement for either print or television, or both. The backstory I crafted for my company revolved around a well-known, traditional Irish brewery situated in Wexford County, Ireland, which was established during the Victorian era. Seeking sustainable practices, they decided to repurpose their Brewery Spent Grains (BSG) into pasta production. My design goal was to achieve a harmonious blend of vintage and contemporary aesthetics. To accomplish this, I drew inspiration from Victorian era mirror frames and from various logos for small design elements. The color palette was distinctly Irish. This project represents my most comprehensive work to date.
Year - 2023
